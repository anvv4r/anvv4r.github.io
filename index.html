ABOUT ME

I am a Full-stack Web Developer, Data Science and Machine Learning enthusiast. I studied web development and data science at Data4You, z.s. in Czechia. I have experience in web development, machine learning, data analysis, and data visualization project. I am open to learn new technologies and enjoy working with people who are as passionate as me.

My journey in IT commenced with a role as a Web Server and Content Manager at Eurolinkmedia International, a Jakarta-based start-up. Since departing in 2010, I have thrived as a web developer on a freelance basis.

Another area where I gained significant experience was in journalism. My journey began in 1997 when I joined Suara Mahasiswa UI. This organization published an annual student magazine and a daily bulletin named "bergerak!" during the 1998 era of Indonesian political history. In 1999, I interned at Bakrie Pension Fund and contributed as a writer for their annual magazine. From 2003 to 2007, I was involved in the publication of several magazines, including Figur Publik Magazine, Depok Metro Magazine, and TaSYA Kids Magazine. My journalistic journey culminated in a teaching role at the State University of Jakarta, where I taught News Writing from 2007 to 2009 as a non-permanent lecturer.

I like photography, music, and walking. If I do not develop, you can find me strolling around Prague doing photo hunt.
